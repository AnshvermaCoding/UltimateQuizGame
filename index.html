How To Play


  1. When Start, A question is appear on the screen with multiple choice options below it.
  2. Click on the option you believe is correct.
  3. Press the "check" button to see if your selected option is correct.
  4. If correct, a point is added to the "Correct" box; if wrong, a point is added to the "Wrong" box.
  5. Use lifelines to get hints or remove incorrect options during the quiz.
  6. Select your preferred category from the options before starting the quiz.
  7. Click the "menu" button for more options.
